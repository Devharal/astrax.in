Celesta.

All night photography competition.

23:00, March 14, 2020 IST - 05:00, March 15, 2020 IST

Click to enlarge.

Problem Statement

In this event, participants can participate as an individual OR in groups of TWO. All the participants/teams have to click pictures that contain atleast one astronomically significant object. Stacking is also allowed.

Timeline

  • The clicking time for the participants/teams is from 11 PM to 5 AM, the next day. The final submission is to be done before 4 PM on the same day.

Judging Criteria

All the participants/teams have to submit TWO images at the time of final submission. For the final score, the cummulative scores of all the TWO submitted images will be taken into consideration. Following points will be considered for judging:

Concept and technical excellence 20%
Composition 35%
Exposure and post-processing 25%
The overall impact,creativity and uniqueness 20%

Rules and Regulations

  • All the participant/teams have to bring their own cameras and equipment and are responsible for it.
  • No equipment shall be provided by the AstraX team or the institute.
  • Every participant/team has to submit TWO images for their final submission.
  • Camera resolution should be atleast 3.2 Mega Pixels.
  • In photo editing; global editing, local editing, photo stacking are allowed. Participants need to submit both, the final image (in JPEG or JPG format) and the raw images used to make the final image.In the case of stacking, the participant can send all or 10 images (whichever is lower) taken at different intervals to cover the whole span .
  • In case of any dispute, the decision of the organizing committee will be final.
  • Please send your submissions to team@astrax.in.
In case of any doubt/concern regarding the event, either call the coordinators or write an email to all@astrax.in.
